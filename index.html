Pen: Flat Preloader (keyframes demo)

by Cordrin Pavel

In this pen the author has taken a demo from Jack Rimel and written it with less code. Plus, he animates with transforms rather than abosolute positioning. Also, he made it to be able to scale so, that it's easy to change the sizes to fit into any project.
